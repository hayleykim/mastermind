Mastermind
Rules
  • For each game, a combination of four pegs will be generated (NO duplicated colors).
  • You have to guess the color combination by clikcing the peg from the bottom row.
  • Choose any color from the pop-up colors (Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Purple), fill up all the 4 pegs & press "Check".
  • For each turn, feedback will be given on the right hand side.
  • Black peg: one of the guessed pegs is correct & is in the right hole.
  • Gray peg: one of the guessed pegs is correct, but is in the wrong hole.
  • Uncolored peg: that color doesn't exist in the color combination. The order of the black & gray pegs does not matter.
  • Keep gussing until you get 4 black pegs on the right hand side. The game ends if you guess the color combination right within 10 turns.
